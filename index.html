About Us

At Gram Sanjivani, we are redefining healthcare with AI-driven medical assistance, making diagnosis and emergency response faster and more efficient. Our platform provides instant health assessments, real-time emergency support and home remedies, ensuring that users receive timely medical care when they need it most.


With our AI-powered Prescription Assistance, users can simply enter their symptoms, and the system will analyze them to generate a preliminary diagnosis and prescription. This is then reviewed and validated by a specialist doctor, ensuring accuracy and reliability.
